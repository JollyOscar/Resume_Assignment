Company Overview

Irving Shipbuilding is one of the most modern shipyards in North America, defined by its pace and appetite for change. Irving Shipbuilding is building more than 20 Arctic and Offshore Patrol Ships and River-Class Destroyers as part of the largest and most complex shipbuilding initiative in Canadian history. If you're curious and motivated by a fast-moving environment and want to contribute to building ships with state-of-the-art naval technology this opportunity is for you.

Position Overview

As a key member of the IT team, you will have a direct impact on the design and enhancements that ensure the digital experience exceeds the expectations of the enterprise.

This position will collaborate with the Infrastructure Design team to deliver products and solutions. They will also support and maintain lifecycle on services and solutions that are in place, as well as satisfying business and operational requirements through the efficient use of internally established and industry best practices. As a leader in technology, this position will drive adherence to leading best practices, standardization, automation, and provide leadership to implementations in an agile framework.

This position requires full-time on-site presence.

All successful applicants must meet requirements for Canadian Controlled Goods Program (CGP), Canadian Government Security clearance, and U.S. International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR).

Key Responsibilities

  • Process improvement of infrastructure support processes for all Tiered support levels
  • Process automation to improve the efficiency and repeatability of operational tasks
  • Provide coaching and mentoring, share technical knowledge, skills, and expertise in an efficient and supportive manner
  • Provide end-to-end technical leadership, planning, workload estimation, organizing, and implementation of products and solutions
  • Execute and support the implementation of infrastructure products and solutions
  • Establish leading practices and policies for implementing, configuring, maintaining, operating, and securing infrastructure
  • Conduct research and provide recommendations on products, services, protocols, and standards to improve operations
  • Monitor and analyze capacity and performance on the environment and recommend enhancements to meet new or changing infrastructure demands
  • Participate in an on-call rotation to serve and support our customers
  • Perform system administration tasks to ensure stability of critical business solutions

  • Core Technical Skills:

    • Knowledge or Experience with Cisco Enterprise Hardware
    • Knowledge or Experience with Citrix
  • Sound Understanding of Industry Leading Practices
  • Attention to Detail (Planning and Prioritization)
  • Effective communication and comfort interacting with management and leadership
